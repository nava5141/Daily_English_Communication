Discussion Points and Conclusions
Topic: If you could change one rule in the world, what would it be?
1. Nikhil Mudhiraj
Points

Nikhil said he would change the rule related to education inequality.

He believes education should be free and of equal quality for everyone.

He pointed out that private schools provide better facilities compared to many government schools.

He also suggested implementing strict anti-corruption rules for government officials.

He discussed issues related to public transport and women’s safety.

He also mentioned that development projects like dams should protect displaced people.

Conclusion

Nikhil concluded that equal education opportunities and transparent governance can create a fair society.

2. Narendar Mekala
Points

Narendar said he would introduce a rule that politicians must pass qualification exams before entering politics.

Different exams could be designed for different levels of leadership like local representatives, MLAs, and ministers.

He said ministers should have knowledge related to their department.

This rule could help reduce unqualified leadership and vote-bank politics.

Conclusion

Narendar concluded that qualified leaders can improve governance and decision-making in the country.

3. Rahul Choppari
Points

Rahul suggested a rule that ensures free and high-quality education for everyone.

He emphasized improving government schools and teaching standards.

He also proposed stronger rules to protect the environment.

He said pollution in air, water, and land must be controlled for future generations.

Conclusion

Rahul concluded that education and environmental protection are key to building a better world.

4. Bondla Akshaya
Points

Akshaya said she would introduce a rule encouraging people to listen and understand each other.

Many conflicts happen because people do not respect different opinions.

She also suggested that society should accept mistakes as part of learning.

This approach would help people build confidence and grow.

Conclusion

Akshaya concluded that better communication and acceptance of mistakes can create a more understanding and peaceful society.

5. Navakumar Kukkaduvva
Points

Navakumar suggested a rule to provide free internet and equal access to technology for everyone.

Internet access helps people learn new skills, apply for jobs, and access government services.

He said technology can help reduce poverty and improve education opportunities.

He also supported the idea of free quality education.

Conclusion

Navakumar concluded that universal internet access can empower people and create more equal opportunities.
