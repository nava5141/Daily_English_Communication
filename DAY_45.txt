Agricultural loss

Government initiatives
🧾 Overall Conclusion
Water crisis is a serious and growing problem in India
Caused by both natural changes and human activities
Affects daily life, agriculture, health, and economy
Solution requires:
Individual responsibility (saving water)
Government action (policies and projects)
Public awareness and education
