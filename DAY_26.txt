🗣️ Group Discussion Topic

Are we losing humanity in the modern world?

👤 Narender’s Points
Technology reduces face-to-face interaction
Materialism makes people money-focused
Parenting is changing (less emotional bonding)
✅ Narender’s Conclusion:

👉 Human connection is decreasing due to overuse of technology and changing values.

👤 Navakumar’s Points
People are becoming more selfish
Less helping behavior in society
Real-life examples of ignoring others in need
✅ Navakumar’s Conclusion:

👉 Humanity is decreasing because people are becoming less caring and more self-centered.

👤 Nikhil’s Points
Gaming and mobile addiction reduce social interaction
People spend more time online than with family
Weakening of social bonds
✅ Nikhil’s Conclusion:

👉 Excessive digital usage is isolating people and affecting relationships.

👤 Rahul’s Points
Some communities and charities still help others
Humanity still exists in many places

👉 Humanity is not completely lost; it still exists but needs encouragement.

🌍 Final Group Conclusion

👉 Yes, humanity is slowly declining due to technology, stress, materialism, and isolation.
👉 However, it is not completely lost—through awareness and small actions, we can rebuild human values.

⭐ Simple Closing Line (for speaking)

👉 “Modern life is making us busy and disconnected, but humanity still exists—we just need to practice it more consciously.”
