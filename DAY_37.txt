🧾 Overall Group Conclusion
The discussion highlighted key national priorities:
Education reform
Healthcare improvement
Employment and economic growth
Rural and agricultural development
Corruption control
Women empowerment
Environmental sustainability
While some responses were creative or unrealistic, most participants focused on practical and development-oriented policies.

The group agreed that:

✅ A successful Prime Minister must balance economic growth, social welfare, technology, and transparency.

🧠 Final One-Line Conclusion (for GD use):

👉 “A strong nation requires balanced leadership that focuses on education, economy, equality, and innovation.”
