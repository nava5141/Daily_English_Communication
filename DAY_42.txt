“If You Had a Superpower, What Would You Do?”

Improve communication

Conclusion:

🔹 Navakumar – Time Travel

Travel to past and future

Conclusion:
👉 His power is about removing regrets and building a happier life.

🔹 Nikhil – Understand Feelings

Understand emotions of others

Conclusion:

Each superpower reflects different priorities:
Productivity (Narender)
Understanding people (Damodar, Nikhil)
Learning and growth (Navakumar, Danu)
Safety and peace (Rahul)
Most powers are not just fantasy—they show real-life needs like time management, emotional understanding, and decision-making.
🧠 Final One-Line Conclusion (for GD):

👉 “Superpowers reflect our real-life desires to improve ourselves, help others, and create a better world.”
