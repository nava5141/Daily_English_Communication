📌 Group-Level Conclusion (Shared by Participants)
Movies are neither completely good nor completely bad.
Their impact depends on:
Viewer mindset
Level of maturity
Ability to distinguish reality from fiction
Movies can:
✅ Inspire, educate, and entertain
❌ Mislead, distract, and promote harmful behavior
🧾 Final Overall Conclusion

Movies have a strong influence on youth, shaping their thoughts, behavior, and attitudes. While they can be a source of inspiration, knowledge, and relaxation, they can also encourage negative habits, unrealistic expectations, and imitation of harmful behavior.

👉 Therefore, the real responsibility lies with the viewer:

Watch movies with awareness
Take only positive lessons
Avoid blindly copying what is shown
