🟢 Topic Name:

“Democracy – Its Meaning, Importance, and Challenges”

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Participants & Their Key Points
🔹 Damodar

Points:

Defined democracy as “government by the people, for the people”
Emphasized voting rights (18+)
Citizens can question the government
Highlighted benefits: freedom, equality, accountability
Also noted issues like money power, caste influence, and unqualified leaders

Conclusion:
👉 Democracy is the best system, but it must be protected from corruption and misuse.

🔹 Navakumar

Points:

Democracy ensures participation and equality
Focused on transparency in government
Raised concerns about corruption and vote-buying
Mentioned instability in political processes

Conclusion:
👉 Democracy works well only when there is transparency and honest leadership.

🔹 Rahul

Points:

Democracy gives power to ordinary people
Enables peaceful change through elections
Supports fundamental human rights
Highlighted real-life political challenges and misuse of promises

Conclusion:
👉 Democracy is powerful because it allows peaceful reform, but requires responsible leadership.

🔹 Darahasini

Points:

Emphasized freedom of speech, religion, and lifestyle
Compared democracy vs autocracy
Explained that democracy allows choice and questioning, unlike controlled systems
Highlighted misuse of welfare schemes for political gain

Conclusion:
👉 Democracy is better than autocracy, but needs ethical governance and awareness.

🧾 Overall Conclusion
Democracy provides:
Freedom and rights
Equality and participation
Accountability and transparency
However, challenges include:
Corruption
Vote-buying and misuse of power
Lack of qualified leadership
The success of democracy depends on:
Aware and responsible citizens
Honest leaders
Active participation in elections and governance
🧠 Final One-Line Conclusion (for GD):

👉 “Democracy is the most powerful system of governance, but its success depends on responsible citizens and ethical leadership.”
