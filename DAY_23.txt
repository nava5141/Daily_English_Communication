Topic: Is Overthinking a Modern Problem?
1️⃣ Rahul – Points

Overthinking existed in the past, but it has increased in modern times.

Social media and modern lifestyle create more pressure and comparison among people.

People constantly analyze situations and worry about outcomes.

Mentioned a news example of a driver going 151 km/h during a live stream, showing how people seek attention and act without thinking.

Overthinking can lead to depression and poor mental health.

Rahul – Conclusion

Overthinking is not new, but modern technology and social media have made it more common and dangerous, affecting mental health.

2️⃣ Bondla Akshaya – Points

Overthinking usually happens when people repeat the same thought again and again.

Even small problems become big problems when we think too much about them.

People should focus on present actions instead of worrying about everything.

Bondla Akshaya – Conclusion

Overthinking can be reduced if people stay focused on the present and avoid unnecessary analysis of small issues.

3️⃣ Navakumar – Points

Overthinking is both a modern problem and a general human problem.

Technology increases information overload and multitasking, which leads to stress.

It can cause anxiety, headaches, depression, and relationship problems.

The internet also has positive uses, like planning for the future and learning new skills.

The impact depends on how people use technology.

Navakumar – Conclusion

Technology is not the problem itself; misusing it leads to overthinking and mental stress.

4️⃣ Nikhil – Points

Social media creates comparison and too many choices, which increases confusion.

Overthinking can be positive or negative.

Positive thinking helps people plan their future and improve life.

Negative overthinking can lead to depression and mental health problems.

Mentioned a celebrity case (Sushant) as an example of mental health struggles.

Nikhil – Conclusion

Overthinking should be controlled and used positively; otherwise, it can harm mental health.

Group Consensus

Overthinking has always existed, but modern technology and social media have intensified it.

It affects people by causing:

Anxiety

Stress

Depression

Relationship issues

Managing thoughts and focusing on the present can reduce the negative effects of overthinking.
