🟢 Topic Name:

“If Humans Lived for 200 Years, What Would They Do?”

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Participants & Their Key Points

🔹 Narender

Preferred a shorter life but planned long-term if living 200 years
Focus on family responsibilities across generations
Invest in assets (house instead of rent)
Prioritize long-term planning over short-term decisions
Enjoy luxuries after fulfilling responsibilities

Conclusion: Practical and family-oriented thinking with strong long-term planning.

🔹 Navakumar

Divide life into phases: learning, earning, and legacy
Long education period and extended working years
Build strong family status and assets
Later life for peace, travel, and charity
Focus on leaving a meaningful legacy

Conclusion: Well-structured life plan with focus on growth, family, and legacy.

🔹 Rahul

Focus on early financial stability and job
Build businesses and large assets
Create a self-sufficient luxury lifestyle
Spend later years with family

Conclusion: Wealth-building and comfortable lifestyle-focused approach.

🔹 Nikhil

Life divided into work phase and simple life phase
Build assets and career early
Later focus on farming and simple living
Strong focus on community service and village development
Interest in politics for public service
Maintain health and independence

Conclusion: Balanced approach combining personal success with social service.

🧾 Overall Conclusion
All participants emphasized:
Long-term planning
Financial stability
Family responsibility
Legacy building
Many also highlighted:
Social contribution
Simple living after success
Personal growth and discipline
🧠 Final One-Line Conclusion (for GD):

👉 “A longer life becomes meaningful only when it is used for growth, responsibility, and contribution to society.”
